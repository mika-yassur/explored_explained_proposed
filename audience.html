Audience

I plan to hold the first (and only planned) iteration of this exhibit in a to-be-determined space in New York City. By choosing to present my work in an exhibit, it makes my work very much for the immediate community—GSAPP and people I plan to invite. Hopefully I can also translate a part of this project (the written component, images, and digital elements of the exhibit) online so that they can be spread more widely.

Creating this project for an even wider public could look like:

  • A zine or digital publication like this
  • A digital narrative atlas
  • An interactive map

I also consider the web archive I am putting together to be a step in this direction. I need to consider what text or explanatory material I will supplement this reference with to make it usable for others.
